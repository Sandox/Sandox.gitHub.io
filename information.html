ABOUT US

     

 
 OUR VISION STATEMENT
Partnering ourselves with one another, clothed with the love of GOD and Christs humility, armed with the sword of the holy spirit which is the Word of GOD, pursing the enemy, recover out possession and restoring man to his rightful godly position

OUR MISSION IS:
To disciple and produce mature Christians who love the Lord and each other, and are equipped to do the work of the ministry To be a fountain where biblical worship abounds in a spiritually refreshing climate To answer seekers questions and to address the issues of life it in the biblical relevant and loving manner To provide an atmosphere where new spiritual horizons are constantly explored, leading many to prosperity and emotional being To enliven the intellect and satisfy the deepest longing of the soul
OUR PURPOSE:
The Purpose of the better Promises Worship Centre is to proclaim and clarify who Jesus is and to make His divinity known to the world
